## Expansion taxonomy (568+ new subjects)

### Category A: Ship Exteriors (~60 subjects)

The visual bible defines ship design per faction. Ships are core to the universe — they're where characters live and work.

**Why it matters:** A model that understands gritty space must recognize that ships are tools, not toys. Firefly vs Star Destroyer. FTL vs Star Citizen.

### Category B: Ship Interiors (~70 subjects)

Where the grit lives. Corridors, crew quarters, engine rooms, cargo holds. This is Alien 1979 territory — the Nostromo interiors are the gold standard.

**Why it matters:** Interior spaces tell the faction story through environmental design. Compact interiors are regulation-clean but impersonal. Veshan interiors are spartan with weapon racks. Reach interiors are cobbled from stolen parts.
