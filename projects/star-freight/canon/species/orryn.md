## Orryn Drift (scaffold)

Grounded does not feature an on-screen Orryn. This entry exists primarily to exercise the `communication_modality.can_lie: no-biological-tell` field Session A designed — the structural axis that makes Orryn diplomacy, subterfuge, and the sealed-mantle stigma subsystem possible in full-game scope.

The Orryn's involuntary chromatophore display is not decoration. It is the species' defining structural trait: they cannot lie without a prosthesis (the sealed mantle), and wearing one carries the gravity of wearing a mask that says *I am hiding what I feel*. Any future Orryn character entry branches from this invariant.
