# Style Constitution

> Define the visual rules that every generated asset must follow.
> Each rule gets an ID, category, and description. The curation scripts score assets against these rules.

## Core Rules

### Silhouette & Shape Language

- **SIL-01**: [Describe the silhouette requirements — what shapes define your style?]
- **SIL-02**: [Describe faction/character shape differentiation]

### Color & Palette

- **COL-01**: [Describe the color rules — palette constraints, faction colors, mood]
- **COL-02**: [Describe what colors are forbidden and why]

### Material & Texture

- **MAT-01**: [Describe material rules — worn vs clean, organic vs synthetic]
- **MAT-02**: [Describe texture expectations — grit level, surface detail]

### Composition & Framing

- **CMP-01**: [Describe framing rules — camera angle, subject placement]

### Mood & Atmosphere

- **MOD-01**: [Describe the emotional tone — tense, serene, chaotic]

## Scoring Dimensions

Assets are scored 0.0–1.0 on each dimension during curation:

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| silhouette | Shape language clarity and faction read |
| palette | Color accuracy and mood alignment |
| material | Surface texture and wear level |
| composition | Framing and visual hierarchy |
| mood | Emotional tone match |
| detail | Level of meaningful detail |
| consistency | Coherence with existing approved assets |
| originality | Avoids generic/stock-art patterns |
